Phase 1
Concept identification
5 min max
Read the problem. Name which HLD concepts are in play (caching? queues? sharding?). If you can't — go to concept lab first.
Phase 2
Estimation
10 min max
QPS, storage, bandwidth. Use round numbers. DAU × events/day ÷ 86400. Don't get exact — get the order of magnitude right.
Phase 3
Design attempt
20 min max
Draw the system: clients → API → services → storage. Apply the concepts you named in phase 1. Stuck? Tell mentor: "Concept: X. My design: Y. Stuck at: Z."
Phase 4
Scale + failure
10 min
What breaks at 10x load? Where is the SPOF? What does the system do when the DB goes down? Graceful degradation.
